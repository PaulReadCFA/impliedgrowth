Gordon Growth Model Equation

This equation calculates the implied growth rate based on current market pricing.

$$\color{#15803d}{g} = \color{#7a46ff}{r} - \frac{\color{#3c6ae5}{Div_{t+1}}}{\color{#b95b1d}{PV_t}} = \color{#7a46ff}{r} - \frac{\color{#3c6ae5}{Div_t(1+g)}}{\color{#b95b1d}{PV_t}}$$
